Product Description

The WindRider High Speed Snow tube has everything you need for a super fast, super fun day on the sledding hill. With a hard bottom, this tube is like the commercial snow tubes, so be prepared for speed. We have a removeable strap to make it easier pull up the hill and 2 handles for holding on while going down.

Please note that because of the solid, super slick bottom you should use caution with young kids. We do not recommend it for small kids on steep, icy hills as the tube can reach speeds in excess of 30MPH.
